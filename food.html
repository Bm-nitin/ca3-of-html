Delicious Cuisine

:

  • Biryani: Lucknowi or Awadhi Biryani is considered the most famous dish of Uttar Pradesh.
  • Chaat: A common and tasty fast food in Uttar Pradesh.
  • Galouti Kebab: A royal delicacy of the nawabs, these kebabs are made of minced meat that disintegrates in your mouth.
  • Petha: A soft, translucent candy made from ash gourd that's popular in Agra.
  • Chole Bhature: A popular street food from North India, where bhatura is a fried bread served with chole, a spicy chickpea curry.
  • Jalebi: A popular Indian dessert made out of flour and sugar syrup, often prepared for festivals like Diwali.
  • Nihari: A Lucknow favorite that was invented in Lucknow and is now famous across the sub-continent.
  • Kachori: A popular breakfast or snack option, Kachori is a deep-fried flatbread stuffed with a savoury filling of lentils, potatoes, or peas.
